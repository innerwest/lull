# Lull — sound credits

Brown, Pink, White and Fan are original procedural recipes, pre-rendered to short
seamless Opus loops.

Four sounds come from third-party sources used under open licences:

- **Rain** — "Heavy Rain" by **D W**, https://freesound.org/people/D%20W/sounds/136971/ —
  **CC BY 3.0** (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/).
  Loudness-normalised and looped. Attribution required by licence.

- **Ocean** — "Waves" from the **Moodist** project, https://github.com/remvze/moodist —
  **Pixabay Content License** (https://pixabay.com/service/license-summary/).
  Loudness-normalised and looped.

- **Wind** — "howling-wind" from the **Moodist** project, https://github.com/remvze/moodist —
  **Pixabay Content License** (https://pixabay.com/service/license-summary/).
  Encoded to Opus/WebM loop.

- **Heartbeat** — "heartbeat-60bpm" by **Ben Holmes**, https://github.com/bencholmes/heartbeat —
  **CC0 1.0** (public domain, https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).
  Cut to a 3-beat loop, heavily low-pass filtered and warmed to sound muffled and womb-like,
  then mixed with a synthesised blood-flow "whoosh" bed (original) for a womb/soother feel.

Attribution for Rain (CC BY 3.0) is surfaced in the in-app About / Credits screen.
If you swap any file, update this attribution accordingly.
